Pakistan is a country in South Asia with a rich cultural heritage and diverse landscapes.
It is home to ancient civilizations like Mohenjo-daro and features both towering mountains and vast deserts.
Islamabad is the capital, while Karachi is the largest city and economic hub.
Geopolitically, it sits at a crucial junction connecting South Asia, Central Asia, and the Middle East.
Pakistan is bordered by Afghanistan, China, India, Iran, and Turkey.
