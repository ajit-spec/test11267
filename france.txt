France is a country located in Western Europe. It is bordered by Belgium, Luxembourg, Germany, Switzerland, Italy, Monaco, Spain, Andorra, and the Bay of Biscay. The capital city of France is Paris, which is also the largest city in the country.
France is known for its rich history, culture, and cuisine.
It is famous for landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum.
France is home to some of the world's most iconic fashion designers, including Chanel, Louis Vuitton, and Dior.
